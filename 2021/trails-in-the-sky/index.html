Trails in the Sky

I recently finished playing The Legend of Heroes: Trails in the Sky games (英雄伝説 空の軌跡).

I finished all the obvious quests (those on the job board and all the doors in the 3rd), but I didn’t 100% the game. Oh well.

My play time according to Steam:

  • FC: 36 hours
  • SC: 45 hours
  • 3rd: 45 hours

My play time according to the game itself, which is different from the real time because of the in-game speedups:

  • FC: 59 hours
  • SC: 86 hours
  • 3rd: 79 hours
